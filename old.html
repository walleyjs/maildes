  • Knowledge Box
    Perfect😍
  • Perfect competition is a market structure that is free from any type of barriers to a free and fair competition between sellers in the market. In essence, competition is at its highest level in this market, and is the direct opposite of a monopoly. The assumptions of perfect competition are such that it is impossible to find outside textbooks (and EDH of course).
    A perfectly competitive market has features such as: the presence of many competing sellers and buyers, easy entry and exit into the market, perfect knowledge of market conditions (such as price, quality of goods, etc) by both buyers and sellers, etc. The features of this market thus ensures that a buyer is only willing to pay as much as the good is worth, and the seller can only sell at a fair price. All the goods in the market are the same, and there is no branding or advertising. As a result neither of the parties can impose a price, instead, the market decides the prices of goods and services.
